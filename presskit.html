Full Description

完整介绍

SnackGuard is an intelligent snack monitoring app for iPhone and Apple Watch that helps you build healthier eating habits — not by counting calories, but by building awareness. The app uses a dual-detection system powered by Bayesian inference, fusing 6+ signals including accelerometer data, phone usage patterns, time-of-day analysis, location proximity, Apple Watch wrist motion, and heart rate monitoring. When it detects you're likely snacking, it sends a personalized reminder in one of three coaching styles: Gentle, Humorous, or Strict. Every time you resist a craving, you're rewarded with a procedurally generated celebration — over 125 billion unique combinations with gyroscope-reactive 3D effects, making each moment of willpower feel special. Track your progress with detailed analytics including weekly trends, snack hotspot maps, eating reason breakdowns, and risk-hour analysis. A comprehensive achievement system offers 533 badges across 8 categories and 5 rarity tiers, from Common to Legendary. Built with privacy at its core: no accounts, no tracking, no ads, no third-party SDKs. All data stays on your device with optional iCloud sync. Available in 33 languages.

SnackGuard 是一款面向 iPhone 和 Apple Watch 的智能零食监控 App，帮你养成更健康的饮食习惯——不计算卡路里，而是建立自我觉察。 App 使用基于贝叶斯推理的双重检测系统，融合 6 种以上信号，包括加速度计数据、手机使用模式、时间规律、地理位置、Apple Watch 手腕动作和心率监测。当检测到你可能在吃零食时，会以三种风格之一发送提醒：温柔型、幽默型或严格型。 每次你成功抵抗诱惑，都会获得一个独一无二的庆祝动画——超过 1250 亿种组合，支持陀螺仪驱动的 3D 效果，让每一次意志力的胜利都有仪式感。 通过详细的数据分析追踪进步，包括每周趋势、零食热点地图、进食原因分析和风险时段预测。完整的成就系统提供 533 个徽章，横跨 8 大类别和 5 个稀有度等级，从普通到传奇。 隐私至上：无需账号、无追踪、无广告、无第三方 SDK。所有数据留在你的设备上，可选 iCloud 同步。支持 33 种语言。

Technical Highlights

技术亮点

  • Bayesian Inference Engine — Dual-service detection (basic 6-signal weighted + advanced self-adaptive Bayesian fusion)
  • CoreMotion Pipeline — 10Hz accelerometer sampling, 12-feature extraction from 3-second windows, CoreML model with rule-based fallback
  • WatchConnectivity — Bidirectional iPhone ↔ Watch sync with offline-safe queued transport
  • Procedural Generation — 250 colors × 250 icons × 200 titles × 200 messages × 50 decorations = 125B+ unique celebrations
  • iCloud KV Sync — NSUbiquitousKeyValueStore with bidirectional merge and conflict resolution
  • JSON-Driven Localization — Custom runtime localization system supporting 33 languages without Xcode .strings
  • 贝叶斯推理引擎 — 双重检测服务（基础 6 信号加权 + 高级自适应贝叶斯融合）
  • CoreMotion 管线 — 10Hz 加速度计采样、3 秒窗口 12 特征提取、CoreML 模型 + 规则回退
  • WatchConnectivity — iPhone ↔ Watch 双向同步，离线安全排队传输
  • 程序化生成 — 250 色 × 250 图标 × 200 标题 × 200 消息 × 50 装饰 = 1250 亿+ 种庆祝组合
  • iCloud KV 同步 — NSUbiquitousKeyValueStore 双向合并与冲突解决
  • JSON 驱动本地化 — 自研运行时本地化系统，支持 33 语言，不依赖 Xcode .strings
